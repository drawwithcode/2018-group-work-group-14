JUST BREATHE.
JUST BREATHE. is a web app that helps you visualize and experience what are the dangers to breathe in an air-polluted area.




O2 (1000 particles)

PARTICULATE MATTER

MUCUS


COMMANDS:

TAP the screen to breath and inhale particles.

SHAKE the device to expell the excess of mucus.
